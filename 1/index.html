    The steps to prepare a sandwich:

  1. Gather Your Ingredients:

    • bread
    • meat
    • cheese
    • veggies
    • mustard
    • mayo
    • ketchup

  2. Prepare Your Workspace:

    • Clean your countertop or cutting board.
    • Have a knife and a plate ready.

  3. Lay Out the Bread:

    • Place two slices of bread on the plate.
    • You can use any type of bread you like, such as white, whole wheat, or a roll.

  4. Spread Condiments:

    • If you want, spread your chosen condiments on one or both slices of bread.

  5. Add Fillings:

    • Layer your fillings on one slice.
    • Start with a protein (like turkey or ham), then add cheese, and finish with veggies (like lettuce, tomatoes, or onions).

  6. Top It Off:

    • Place the second slice of bread on top to complete your sandwich.

  7. Cut the Sandwich (Optional):

    • If you prefer, cut the sandwich in half or into quarters for easier eating.

  8. Serve and Enjoy:

    • Your sandwich is ready!
    • Serve it with chips, pickles, or whatever sides you like.
